Essential Equipment

Before embarking on your mountain adventure, make sure you have these essentials:

Hiking Equipment Checklist

  • Footwear
    • Hiking boots
    • Wool socks (multiple pairs)
    • Camp shoes/sandals
  • Clothing
    • Moisture-wicking base layers
    • Insulating mid layers
    • Waterproof outer layer
    • Hat and gloves
  • Equipment
    • Backpack (40-60L)
    • Trekking poles
    • Headlamp

Expert Trail Guides

Our certified mountain guides can help you navigate the most challenging terrains.

Guide Certification Levels

Level 1 - Trail Guide
Qualified to lead day hikes on marked trails in good weather conditions.
Level 2 - Mountain Guide
Experienced in overnight trips and moderate technical terrain.
Level 3 - Expedition Leader
Certified for technical climbing, high altitude expeditions, and rescue operations.
