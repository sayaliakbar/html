Bonus Exercise: Combining Elements

Challenge: Travel Destination Guide

Create a comprehensive travel guide for a destination of your choice by combining all the HTML elements you've learned this week. Your guide should include:

  1. A main heading and introduction paragraph
  2. A table of contents with anchor links to sections below
  3. At least 3 images with proper alt text and captions
  4. An unordered list of attractions to visit
  5. An ordered list of top 5 restaurants
  6. A definition list explaining local terms/phrases
  7. A table comparing hotels (with columns for name, price range, rating, and amenities)
  8. Links to external travel resources and an email contact link
  9. A simple image map highlighting key areas in your destination
  10. Proper semantic structure and organization

This exercise challenges you to integrate everything you've learned about links, images, lists, and tables into a unified and useful web page.
